Rules

  • Rules for using the sports room
  • Only residents of the dormitories Gret-Palucca-Street 9 and 11 are allowed to use the gym.
  • Use only with clean shoes.
  • Bring your own towel.
  • Be mindful of your surroundings.
  • Keep the walls clean. Don't lean weights nor yourself against them.
  • Do not place weights directly on the floor. Use the mats.
  • Re-rack your plates and your dumbbells.
  • Clean items after workout.
  • Open the windows 15 minutes before end of workout. Close the windows after workout.
  • Turn off lights after workout.
